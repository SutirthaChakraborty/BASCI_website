Our Sports Legacy

Welcome to the Bengal Amateur Sports Club (BASCI) ‚Äì Ireland's first registered sports club initiative founded by the Bengali community. BASCI was established to unite Bengali sports enthusiasts and the broader Indian community living in Ireland through a shared passion for sports. Our club serves as a vibrant platform for individuals who love sports, fostering a supportive community where members can connect, compete, and grow together.At BASCI, we believe that sports not only promote physical well-being but also build lasting friendships and a strong sense of belonging. We are proud to create an inclusive space where every member feels welcomed, valued, and part of something greater. Join us as we celebrate the joy of sports and the spirit of community!
