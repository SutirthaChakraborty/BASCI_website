Who We Are

More Than Just a Sports Club

BASCI (Bengal Amateur Sports Club of Ireland) is not your ordinary sports club — we're a family of passionate individuals who believe in the unifying power of sports. Based in the heart of Dublin, Ireland, our club brings together people from all walks of life who share a common love for athletic pursuits.

Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just starting your sporting journey, BASCI welcomes you with open arms. We organize regular football matches, badminton sessions, and various sporting events that bring our community together.
