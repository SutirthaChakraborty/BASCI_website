Roy Sinha Memorial Derby Cup

Our flagship football tournament that honors the legacy of Roy Sinha. This annual event brings together teams from across the community for an exciting day of competitive football, sportsmanship, and camaraderie. The tournament features multiple team matches culminating in a thrilling final.
