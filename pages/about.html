About BASCI

Discover the story, mission, and values that drive Bengal Amateur Sports Club of Ireland

Our Story

A Community Built on Passion

BASCI – Bengal Amateur Sports Club of Ireland – was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to bring people together through the universal language of sports. What started as a small group of sports enthusiasts gathering for weekend football matches has grown into one of Dublin's most vibrant community sports organizations.

Our founders believed that sports could bridge cultural gaps, foster friendships, and create a sense of belonging for the Bengali community in Ireland. Today, while we remain deeply connected to our Bengali heritage, BASCI welcomes members from all backgrounds who share our love for sports and community.

From organizing local football matches to hosting prestigious tournaments like the Roy Sinha Memorial Derby Cup, we've created countless memories and lasting bonds. Our journey continues as we expand our sporting offerings and strengthen our community ties.

Our Mission

To promote sports and physical fitness within the community, fostering an environment where individuals of all ages and skill levels can participate, compete, and excel. We aim to build a strong, connected community through regular sporting events, training sessions, and social gatherings.

Our Vision

To become Ireland's leading community sports club that celebrates diversity, promotes healthy living, and creates lasting connections through sports. We envision a future where BASCI serves as a model for inclusive, community-driven sports organizations across the country.

Our Values

Unity: Bringing people together through sports.
Inclusivity: Welcoming all, regardless of background or skill level.
Excellence: Striving for the best in everything we do.
Community: Building lasting relationships and supporting one another.
