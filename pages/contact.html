FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about BASCI

How do I become a member?

You can become a member by contacting us through this form, visiting our Facebook group, or joining us during one of our regular Sunday football sessions. Annual membership is €50 for adults and €30 for students.

What sports do you offer?

We currently offer Football, Cricket, and Badminton. Football is our flagship activity with weekly Sunday sessions. Cricket is played during summer, and Badminton sessions are held monthly on Saturdays.

Do I need prior experience?

No prior experience is required! Our club welcomes players of all skill levels. Whether you're a beginner or experienced player, you'll find a welcoming community here.

Can non-Bengalis join?

Absolutely! While our club has Bengali roots, we welcome sports enthusiasts from all backgrounds. Our goal is to promote sports and build community regardless of nationality or ethnicity.

What events do you organize?

We host several annual events including the Roy Sinha Memorial Cup, BASCI-NCC Friendship Cup, Annual Awards Night, and Badminton Tournament. We also organize regular training sessions and friendly matches.

How can I sponsor or partner with BASCI?

We welcome sponsors and partners! Please reach out via the contact form above or email us directly. We offer various sponsorship packages for our events and can discuss custom partnership opportunities.
